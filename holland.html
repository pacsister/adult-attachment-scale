職業興趣測試（Holland RIASEC） Career Interest Test (Holland RIASEC)

John Holland（1971）；部份內容取自勞工處擇業輔導組。共三部份、各 12 題。 John Holland (1971); adapted in part from Hong Kong Labour Department career guidance materials. Three parts, 12 items each.
完成後會按「原本計分表」把每部份指定題號加總到 R/I/A/S/E/C。 After you finish, the page sums the specified item numbers for each type (R/I/A/S/E/C) based on the original scoring sheet.

R
實務性Realistic
得分：Score: —
特徵：Characteristics:
  • 喜歡以物、機械工作等為對象Prefer working with objects, tools, machines, and practical tasks
  • 有規則的、明確的、有序的、系統的活動Like structured, clear, orderly, and systematic activities
  • 須具備與手、機械、電氣技術等有關的能力Require abilities related to hands-on, mechanical, or electrical skills
  • 性格往往具體、順應、札實，但社交能力比較缺乏的Often practical and down-to-earth; social skills may be relatively weaker
合適的工作類別：Suitable work categories:
  • 技能性及技術性工作為主Mainly skilled and technical work
I
考究性Investigative
得分：Score: —
特徵：Characteristics:
  • 分析型、有智慧的、有探究心的、內省的Analytical, intellectual, inquisitive, and reflective
  • 主要透過實驗或調查、觀察及分析和歸納問題Solve problems through investigation, observation, analysis, and inference
  • 將科技實際應用在現實的生活上Apply scientific knowledge and technology to real life
合適的工作類別：Suitable work categories:
  • 與研究及應用科學有關，研究的對象可以是自然或社會經濟現象Research and applied science (natural or socio-economic topics)
A
藝術性Artistic
得分：Score: —
特徵：Characteristics:
  • 具有想像、衝動、直覺、無秩序、情緒化、理想化、有創意、不重實際的Imaginative, intuitive, creative; may be less structured or practical
  • 須充分發揮自己的藝術天分、創作意念和想像力Need opportunities to express artistic talent, ideas, and imagination
合適的工作類別：Suitable work categories:
  • 包括音樂、美術及設計等工作Music, fine arts, design, and related work
S
社會性Social
得分：Score: —
特徵：Characteristics:
  • 合作、友善、助人、負責任、圓滑、善予社交言談、善解人意的Cooperative, friendly, helpful, responsible, socially skilled, empathetic
合適的工作類別：Suitable work categories:
  • 醫療保健、教育、傳播及社會福利等工作Healthcare, education, communication, and social welfare
E
企業性Enterprising
得分：Score: —
特徵：Characteristics:
  • 喜歡冒險、精力充沛、善予社交、自信心強Adventurous, energetic, sociable, confident
  • 強烈關注目標的追求、喜歡從事為獲得利益而操縱、驅動別人的活動Goal-focused; enjoy persuading, influencing, and leading others to achieve gains
  • 主導性及說服能力強Strong leadership and persuasion skills
合適的工作類別：Suitable work categories:
  • 適合從事領導或企業策劃管理、銷售、貿易Leadership/management, business planning, sales, trade
C
常規性Conventional
得分：Score: —
特徵：Characteristics:
  • 順從、謹慎、保守、實際、穏重、有效率、善予自我控制Careful, practical, steady, efficient, and self-controlled
  • 喜從事記錄、整理檔案資料、操作辦公室文儀、處理數據、有系統及有條理的活動Prefer recording/organizing data, clerical tasks, and systematic, orderly activities
合適的工作類別：Suitable work categories:
  • 適合會計師、一般事務、文書工作等Accounting, general administration, clerical work
